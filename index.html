Welcome to Busoga Girls Code

We are a group of passionate, tech-driven girls from the Busoga region on a mission to bridge the technology gap by empowering our peers through digital skills.

Join the Movement

Our Mission

To empower young girls with technology skills and inspire them to become creators, problem solvers, and leaders in the digital world.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every girl in our community has access to technology education, resources, and opportunities to thrive in the tech space.

Why Girls in Tech?

In many communities, girls are left behind in technology. At Busoga Girls Code, we believe that bridging this gap is not only fair — it's necessary. Technology drives the future, and we want to ensure girls are part of that future.

We aim to:

  • Encourage more girls to pursue careers in technology
  • Offer hands-on coding workshops
  • Mentor girls through tech projects
  • Raise awareness about digital literacy

What We Do

Our team has acquired strong skills in web design using HTML and CSS, game development with Scratch, beginner robotics, and machine learning. We use these skills to teach others, host community sessions, and build projects that raise awareness about technology.

Through our website and offline training, we offer:

  • Free tutorials for beginners
  • In-person coding sessions
  • Inspirational talks from girl coders
  • Real projects built by girls

Be Part of the Change

If you’re a girl who’s curious, creative, or just wants to explore the tech world – we welcome you! Together we can build something amazing.
